About us

Meet Karen, from Kolkata 🙋

Karen really wants to buy her favourite pair of Nike Air Max 90 in the colour Dark Driftwood. She has been eyeing a deal on her favourite shopping website, only to find her size repeatedly out of stock. Karen observes that her dear pair is being restocked on the mornings of Tuesday, Friday, and Sunday. Karen, after intense calculations, sets her alarm clock accordingly. By this time, the deal has ended. Karen is sad. Karen did not know about our Autobuy and price drop alert services. Don't be a Karen. Choose wisely!

Everyone has felt the need for rush-hour aid, and our team felt the need to create a feature to help you combat feeling discouraged when you see phrases like "size out of stock" and "sale has ended". We spent hours brainstorming a hassle-free, tangible solution which we believe could make a difference. Millennials, we got you, GenZ; we heard you. The Autobuy and the price drop features are the by-products of the hypotheses we carried out at a personal level; the fuel to which was to make the time we spend on the shopping website, and in turn that of many like ourselves, a bit more efficient.

  • Stressing out about having to remember to clear your cart before the deal ends? Tell us about it when the popup appears. We will notify you or buy it for you.
  • Your favourite pair is also someone else's favourite? Let us buy it for you when it is back in stock.
  • Missed a sale? Try adjusting your desired price, and we will purchase it for you when the price drops. Not sure if you want to buy those sneakers? Wishlist it! We will notify you.
