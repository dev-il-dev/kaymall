KOTAK's AUTOBUY

Price Drop Alerts

The price drop feature of the Kotak Autobuy can notify the customers about the price drop and sales on the product they have suscribed to recieve these updates about. These notifications can be customized to be in app notifications, priority notifications as well as email alerts.



AutoBuy

The AutoBuy feature helps the users to place orders for the products that they are not willing to buy at the current price but will be willing to buy it if the price reaches to a certain desired value. The system asks the users to select the desired prices and a shipping address and places order as soon as the prices hit that value on click of a single button.



Order Out of Stock Products

Using Kotak's AutoBuy, you can also place order or suscribe to price drop alerts for items that are currently out of stock- the process is simple, click on Kaywish and the feature would ask you whether you want to place order for the specific product once it comes back in stock. The user selects an address and we place the order as soon as the product comes back in stock.
