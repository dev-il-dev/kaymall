Our Services

Price Drop Alert

Notifies you about the price drop and sales on the product you have suscribed to recieve these updates about, in the popup. These notifications can be customized to be in app notifications, priority notifications as well as email alerts.



Autobuy

The system will ask you to select the desired prices and a shipping address in the and places order as soon as the prices hit that value, on the click of a single button.

Stock refill alert

Using Kotak's AutoBuy, you can also place order or subscribe to price drop alerts for items that are currently out of stock- the process is simple, click on Kaywish and the feature would ask you whether you want to place order for the specific product once it is re-stocked. Select an address and we place the order as soon as the product comes back in stock.
